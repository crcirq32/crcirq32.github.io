Careers in Tech

There are so many careers in IT, network security, system administration, bug bounty, big data, accounting, software development, bioinformatics, the list goes on and on.

Paying for School

Technically, someone can get a job in IT with enough work. If you are a dedicated individual than you can teach yourself most tools of the trade. If you are more of a hands on or visual learner some prefer to get certificates or degrees in computer science, or in a like field. A great way to pay for school could be to search if the state has any stipend avaliable. It's worth a shot, go to the FASFA website and apply FASFA.
